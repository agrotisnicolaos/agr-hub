The base · architecture

How the launchpad is built

agr-launchpad was created around one idea: give you a productive starting point that assists you without getting in your way. Rather than a pile of tools, it's an arrangement — proven pieces from credible makers, wired so they cooperate. You get a safety net and stay in control.

Use it at any depth

The launchpad meets you wherever you are. You can stay shallow and just talk, or go as deep as you like — nothing forces a level on you.

Depth 1

Just talk

Open the folder and describe what you want. The base assists you automatically — you never touch a setting.

Depth 2

Reach for tools

Call a skill like /grill-me, run /code-review, or start a live notebook when you want more.

Depth 3

Customize & extend

Add your own skills, tune the rulebook, and install packs. Shape it into exactly your setup.

It grows with you — packs & hubs

The base stays deliberately small. When you start a particular kind of project, you add a pack — a bundle of project-specific skills, agents, and tools — with one command. Packs come from this hub, agr·hub, and can come from other hubs and marketplaces too. Nothing bloats the base; you opt into exactly the capability you want, when you want it.
